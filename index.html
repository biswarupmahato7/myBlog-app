Exploring Manali With Me

Best place in Manali

  • Solang Valley
  • Hidimba Devi Temple
  • Jogini Waterfall
  • Bese River
  • Solang Nulla

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in India’s northern Himachal Pradesh state. It has a reputation as a backpacking center and honeymoon destination. Set on the Beas River, it’s a gateway for skiing in the Solang Valley and trekking in Parvati Valley. It's also a jumping-off point for paragliding, rafting and mountaineering in the Pir Panjal mountains, home to 4,000m-high Rohtang Pass

The Secret of Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish renowned for its rich and creamy texture. Originating from Delhi, it consists of marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato-based curry, enriched with butter and cream. The dish is characterized by its vibrant orange-red color and a blend of aromatic spices, including garam masala, cumin, and coriander. Typically served with naan or rice, butter chicken offers a delightful balance of savory, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors, making it a favorite in Indian cuisine worldwide.

Olympic Games Paris 2024

The 2024 Summer Olympics, officially known as the Games of the XXXIII Olympiad, will be held in Paris, France. Scheduled to take place from July 26 to August 11, 2024, this will mark the third time Paris has hosted the event, with previous games held in 1900 and 1924. The Paris 2024 Olympics will feature new sports, including surfing, skateboarding, and sport climbing, reflecting the evolving nature of the Games. With a strong emphasis on sustainability and innovation, the Paris Games aim to set new standards for environmental responsibility and community engagement. The event promises to celebrate the spirit of athletic excellence and international unity in the heart of one of the world's most iconic cities
